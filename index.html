Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Spark Extensions ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome extensions handle your information.

Information We Collect

Our extensions operate primarily on-device. We collect minimal data:

  • Email address (only if you create a Pro account)
  • Payment information (processed securely by Stripe — we never see your card details)
  • Extension settings and preferences (stored locally in your browser)

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Your browsing history or visited URLs
  • Page content you view
  • Personal usage data beyond what's stored locally on your device

Local Storage

Focus streaks, game scores, and settings are stored locally using Chrome's storage API. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly share it.

Third-Party Services

We use Stripe for payment processing. Stripe's privacy policy applies to payment data. We do not share your data with any other third parties.

Children's Privacy

Our extensions include a Parent Dashboard feature designed for parental oversight of minors' usage. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.
